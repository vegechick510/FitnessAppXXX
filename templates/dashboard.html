Fitness Recipes

Grilled Chicken Salad
A high-protein, low-carb salad with greens, grilled chicken, and a light dressing.
Marinate chicken breast with olive oil, salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs.
Grill the chicken on medium heat for 6-8 minutes on each side.
Chop fresh greens (lettuce, spinach, etc.) and add cherry tomatoes, cucumbers.

Protein Smoothie Bowl
A delicious smoothie bowl with protein powder, fresh fruits, and nuts.
Blend 1 scoop of protein powder with 1 frozen banana, 1 cup frozen berries.
Top with fresh fruits, granola, and nuts (e.g., almonds or walnuts).

Quinoa Veggie Stir-fry
A healthy stir-fry with quinoa, broccoli, bell peppers, and tofu.
In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté diced onions and minced garlic.
Add chopped broccoli, bell peppers, and tofu cubes, and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
Mix in the cooked quinoa and season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
